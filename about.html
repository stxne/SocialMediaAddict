ABOUT US

Social Media Addict is a website dedicated to bringing awareness to social media addiction. We want to help people become more aware of their relationship with social media and promote a healthier experience for those who consistently use it.

OUR MISSION

Our mission at Social media Addict is to give avid social media users resources to help and educate them on social media addiction delivered with trust, thoughtfulness and understanding.

OUR VISION

Our vision is to be the go to site and resource for all things relating to social media usage and addiction. We aim to help users become more conscious of how they use social social media and the way it impacts their livelihood.
