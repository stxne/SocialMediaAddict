WHAT IS
SOCIAL MEDIA
ADDICTION?

Social media addiction is a behavioral addiction that is defined by being overly concerned about social media, driven by an uncontrollable urge to log on to or use social media, and devoting so much time and effort to social media that it impairs other important life areas.

SYMPTOMS OF SOCIAL MEDIA ADDICTION

Symptoms of social media addiction may look like social media usage cutting into your work time, immediately checking your phone when you get a notification, constantly monitoring your posts to see how mant likes you get, chacking your accounts first thing when you wake up and right before bed, and by measuring life events by how post worthy they are. Visit our resource page for more information.

TREATMENTS FOR SOCIAL MEDIA ADDICTION

One of the best ways to break an addiction to social media is to set boundaries and reduce screen-time; however, if the addiction is too severe you may require professional help. Visit our resource page for more information on how to treat social media addiction.
